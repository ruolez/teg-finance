Setup Guide

1
Enable 2-Step Verification

Go to your Google Account > Security > 2-Step Verification and enable it.

2
Generate App Password

In Security > 2-Step Verification > App Passwords, create a new app password for "Mail".

3
Configure Settings

Enter your Gmail address and the 16-character app password in the form.

4
Test Configuration

Click "Send Test Email" to verify everything works correctly.
